How to Use Meal Planner Pro

Getting Started

1. Use the planner settings to set your desired duration and meals per day.

2. Click on any meal slot in the calendar to add or edit a meal.

3. Use meal templates to quickly add common meals.

Features

• Shopping List: Ingredients from your meals automatically populate the shopping list.

• Meal Templates: Save your favorite meals as templates for quick reuse.

• Export/Import: Save your meal plans as JSON files and import them later.

• Print: Print your meal plan or shopping list.
